Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin mconsisting of a mix of flat dough base, tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll out the dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake until crust is golden.
