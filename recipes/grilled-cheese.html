Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet satisfying meal.

Crispy bread and melted cheese make it a timeless classic.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between unbuttered sides.
  3. Cook in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Flip when golden brown.
  5. Cook until cheese is melted.
