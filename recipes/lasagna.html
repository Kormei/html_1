Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese.

This hearty dish is perfect for family dinners and leftovers taste even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Cook ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let rest before serving.
